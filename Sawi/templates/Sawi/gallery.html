Welcome To Sofwat Arabic and Islamic Studies Institute

Zawiyat Sofwat El-Islam established in 1985, now situated in 7, Zawiyat Sofwat Crescent, Ajegunle Ilo, Lagos State, Nigera

The institute bulit to enable muslims learn to read and write in arabic and fundamentals of the deen (Islam). Among the Institue various aim and objectives is to eradicate ignoranc among muslim communities and make stronger the bond of brotherhood in our society.

The Propietor in person of Sheik Al-islam along side his management team oversee the daily runing of the institution
